About Me

Hello! I'm Jaden Rogers, a game designer and artist passionate about creating immersive worlds and compelling visuals. With a background in digital art and game design, I specialize in environment art, weapon design, and graphic design for games. My goal is to bring stories to life through compelling visuals and innovative design.

I have experience working with in both small and large teams, collaborating with writers, programmers, and artists to create cohesive and engaging game experiences. I thrive in environments where creativity and collaboration intersect, and I am always eager to learn new skills and techniques to enhance my craft.

You can find me exploring new games, sketching or blocking out new ideas, or diving into sci-fi and fantasy worlds for inspiration.
